Punk Science Studios Inc.

Punk Science Studios Inc. was a video game, software development and media company founded in 2015 by Darryl Wright in Halifax, Nova Scotia. For over 2 years, the team explored creative ideas, produced games, apps, art and even medical R&D on the front lines of cancer research.

The business was built on the principles of technical excellence, talented people and a corporate culture that put team chemistry and personal development first. While the business has been shelved indefinitely and is not taking on any new projects, you can still find Darryl on most social media outlets.
